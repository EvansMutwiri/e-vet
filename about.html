About us

Welcome to
E-vet Center

E-vet Center has been providing
compassionate and quality veterinary care in
Nairobi and it's surrounding. Each year our
veterinary team attends a two weeks programme
of continuing education to provide your animals with
the most current medical and surgical care needs.

we are unique in that we provide medical care to multiple species in the greater Nairobi City.

We are a group eight vets.some of our veterinarians specialize in the care of dogs and cats and the other veterinarians specialize in the care of livestock.

Our Vision

To build a lifelong relationship for healthy pets
by administering the best possible medical care.


Our Mission

To be the leading center that provides quality services
and care to our animals and create a good environment
for both the owners and their animals.
